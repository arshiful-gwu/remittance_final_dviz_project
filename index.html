Introduction

Abstract

A sum of money sent, especially by mail, in payment for goods or services or as a gift is called remittance and as more and more people move from developing countries to the developed ones for the purpose of education, work and etc., the amount of remittance transfer is seen to be increasing according to the data found in World Bank. Many countries do not track funds that are sent or received which makes it difficult to track remittance payments worldwide. This project aims to visually show the remittance transfer, compare them, and find trends using geographic and interactive charts.
